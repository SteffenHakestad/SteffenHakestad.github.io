CASE HISTORY 3

Case study for North Sea Operator on Norwegian Platform using 1 x 150 bar Dynamic Desander including Control Container, total flowline package for the entire operation, bypass valves and ESD.

Background

The objective was to clean out Sandaband Plug in Tubing and A-annulus in preparation for permanently plug and abandon.

Solution

1 x 150 bar Dynamic Desander including Control Container, total flowline package for the entire operation, bypass valves and ESD.

Cooperated with Coiled Tubing (CT).

Challenges

Unknown amount of Sandaband.

Large flowline package.

Results

Successfully received 52 600 kg of Sandaband.

Well was treated continuously and separated solids without any interruption of well flow.

Completed the operation with no HSE incidents.

Zero downtime.
