THE CONCEPT

Unlike other single or dual-flow solids removal systems using conventional nozzle / liner with static or semi-static cyclone separation principles – The DDS™ has no inlet nozzle or liner, thus removing traditional equipment erosion hazards and HSE risks from the well site. We separate solids but never compromise SAFETY.

By use of a novel impellor the Dynamic Desander System generates a powerful centrifuge inside the separation chamber which discharges solids with supreme efficiency in all applications.

The units are fully autonomous with real-time telemetry on all process critical parameters such as weight of solids, pressures, temperatures, and equipment diagnostics, thus enabling the possibility for remote operation.

Each unit consists of an upper separation chamber and a lower collection chamber. The solids are separated in the upper chamber and gravitate into the lower chamber. The entire process is fully automated and the collection chamber can be emptied whilst continually separating solids and without closing in the well flow.

The power of the centrifuge cleans the separated solids, typically to within the strictest environmental compliance standards. No additional cleaning equipment or treatment is normally required, which significantly reduces the environmental impact on location and simplifies the final disposal, thus saving costs.
