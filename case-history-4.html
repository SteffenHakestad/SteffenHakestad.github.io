CASE HISTORY 4

Case study for North Sea Operator on Norwegian Platform using 2 x 150 bar Dynamic Desander and well control equipment such as remote choke, Control Container, iron, bypass valves and ESD.

Background

The well was shut-in due to low pressure to produce and flowline plugged with sand 14.09.2013.

Objective was to clean out sand down to 4 965 mMD.

Possibility of scale and expected large particles up to 20 mm.

Solution

2 x 150 bar Dynamic Desander System (DDSâ„¢) and well control equipment such as remote choke, Control Container, iron, bypass valves and ESD.

Cooperated with Coiled Tubing (CT).

Challenges

Unknown amount of solids.

Received large particles up to 52 mm.

Pressure build up due to blockage in well.

Results

Successfully received 2 519 kg of solids.

Well was treated continuously and separated solids without any interruption of well flow.

Completed the operation with no HSE incidents.
